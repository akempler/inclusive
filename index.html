Issue Categories

Vision Impairment

Complete and partial blindness

Color blindness

Poor vision

Avoid:

Small text

All caps

Tight leading and kerning

Color-coding as only means of conveying information or indicating an action

Provide:

Text size overrides

Contrast/color overrides

Accessibility Options:

Motor Impairment

Poor motor skills like hand movements

Not able to use the mouse

Avoid:

Tightly grouped clickable elements

Small clickable elements

Provide:

Site navigability by keyboard only

Tab through content in logical order

Cognitive Disability

Learning difficulties

Poor memory

Unable to understand complex scenarios

Avoid:

Long, dense content

Long sentences and paragraphs

Provide:

Content summaries

Page table of contents

Break long content with headings and images

Focused content

Literacy Impairments

Reading problems

Low english proficiency

Provide:

Content summaries

Page table of contents

Content is written plain and simple

Hearing Disability

Auditory deafness

Hearing impairments

Provide:

Captions for videos and audio files

Other Disabilities

Epilepsy

Autism Spectrum

Avoid:

Flashing items

Quickly rotating items on by default

Very high contrast colors

Provide:

Obvious ways to stop auto-rotating content

Ability to change contrast/colors
